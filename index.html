Privacy Policy

Calmset — Effective March 2026

Introduction

Calmset is developed and published by HekaPhysio. This policy explains what data the app handles, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

Calmset is an offline-first application. It does not require user accounts, has no cloud sync, and stores all personal data locally on your device in a SQLite database. We cannot access, read, or retrieve your local data.

Calmset is available on iOS, Android, and Apple Watch (bundle ID: com.gymtimer.app).

Data We Collect

All of the following data is stored exclusively on your device. None of it is transmitted to our servers.

  • Timer state — remaining seconds, timer state, current set index. Retained only until the session ends.
  • Training presets — built-in and user-created presets. Retained until you delete them.
  • Session history — timestamps, training mode, number of sets, rest duration, and completion percentage. Auto-purged after 365 days.
  • Install ID — a randomly generated UUID created by the app. It is not linked to your Apple ID, Google account, or any personal identifier. Retained until you uninstall or reset the app.
  • Notification settings — your notification preferences. Retained until you change them.
  • Theme & language preferences — retained until you change them.

Third-Party Services

Calmset uses the following third-party service:

  • RevenueCat — used solely for in-app purchase validation. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and transaction receipts. It does not receive your name, email, or any other personal information. See the RevenueCat Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

Planned Features NOT YET ACTIVE

The following features are planned for future releases. They are not currently active in the app. This section will be updated before each feature launches.

  • HealthKit / Health Connect integration — will share derived workout summaries only (no raw health samples). Requires explicit user opt-in. Data retained locally for 365 days.
  • Crash diagnostics — will collect stack traces, app version, and OS version to help us fix bugs. Retained for 30 days.
  • Product analytics — will collect anonymized usage events (e.g., feature adoption, session counts) with no personal identifiers. Retained for 180 days.

Data We Don't Collect

Calmset does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following — now or in any planned update:

  • Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, or mailing address
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • GPS or location data
  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA / AAID)
  • IMEI, device serial number, or hardware identifiers
  • Clipboard contents
  • Raw health samples (heart rate, steps, etc.)

Your Rights

Because Calmset stores data locally on your device and does not maintain user accounts or a cloud backend, your rights are straightforward:

  • Access & deletion of local data — uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data. You can also delete individual presets and history within the app.
  • RevenueCat data deletion — to request deletion of purchase data held by RevenueCat, email us at info@heka.rehab and we will process the request on your behalf.
  • No data sold — we do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. This applies under both the GDPR and the CCPA.

Children's Privacy

Calmset is not designed for or directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with data, please contact us at info@heka.rehab and we will take steps to address the situation.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
